2000s fashion is back

Trends we tried to erase from our memories are returning to the catwalks and to our wardrobes. Velour suits, skinny cardigans and low-waisted jeans. ..... Take a look back at the most impressive fashion trends of the 2000s. Vintage style is back!

81st Venice International Film Festival: Celebrity Looks

The 81st Venice International Film Festival took place on the Italian island of Lido from August 28 to September 7. Taylor Russell in Loewe and Tiffany & Co. jewelry, Cate Blanchett in Louis Vuitton, Emily Ratajkowski in Gucci took to the red carpet. The stars chose a black, scarlet, white and silver palette to dazzle the audience, as well as flawless diamonds.

New collection of the H&M brand

The next part of H&M Studio was inspired by the relaxed style codes of the 1970s, updated with a carefree, modern essence. “Spring/summer 2024 expresses a yearning for adventure,” says Linda Wickell, conceptual designer at H&M Studio. “We imagined a 1970s musician on the road, a creative soul who travels light, picking up pieces for his wardrobe along the way.”

Vintage
A stylized trend in fashion, especially in clothing and household items, that focuses on the revival of fashion trends of past generations and eras.
Venice International Film Festival
An annual film festival held in Venice, Italy. It is the world's oldest film festival and one of the "Big Five" international film festivals worldwide.
